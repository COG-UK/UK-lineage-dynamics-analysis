Estimated importation lags for UK transmission lineages of different sizes. Importation lag is the waiting time between importation date and the TMRCA of the sampled genomes in the transmission lineage. Detection lag is the waiting time from the importation date to the sampling time of the oldest (first) sampled genome in the transmission lineage.
